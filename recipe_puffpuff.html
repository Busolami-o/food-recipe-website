Puff Puff

Puff Puff is a soft, golden, deep-fried dough ball that’s slightly sweet. It’s a popular Nigerian snack often served at parties, street stalls, or breakfast. Crispy on the outside and fluffy inside, it’s a simple comfort food that’s loved by kids and adults alike.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp yeast
  • Warm water
  • A pinch of salt
  • Oil for frying

How To Prepare

  1. Mix flour, sugar, salt, and yeast in a bowl.
  2. Add warm water gradually until the batter is thick but stretchy.
  3. Cover and allow to rise for 45 to 60 minutes.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pot.
  5. Scoop batter into hot oil and fry until golden brown.
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve warm.
