Crispy Chicken Wings

Golden, crunchy, and seasoned to perfection, these wings are fried until crispy on the outside while remaining juicy and tender inside. Perfect as a snack or party appetizer.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings
  • pepper
  • Corn Flour
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt

How To Prepare

  1. Wash wings and pat dry.
  2. Season with salt,Seasoning cubes, garlic, ginger and pepper.
  3. Add 1-2 tbsp cornflour and mix.
  4. Heat oil and fry on medium heat until golgen and crisy.
  5. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.
