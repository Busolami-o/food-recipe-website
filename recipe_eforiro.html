Efo Riro(Vegetable soup)

A hearty Nigerian soup made with fresh leafy vegetables, palm oil, assorted meats, and spices. Rich in flavor and nutrients, it’s typically served with rice or swallow like pounded yam.

Ingredients

  • Ugwu
  • Blended pepper mix with onoins
  • Meat, Fish,Ponmo,Stockfish and any other protain of your choice
  • Seasoning cubes and salt
  • Palm oil
  • Locust Bans

How To Prepare

  1. Boil meat with seasoning.
  2. Heat palm oil, add iru, then pepper mix and fry well.
  3. Add meat, stock, crayfish, and cook 5 to 7 mins.
  4. Add washed vegetables.
  5. Stir and cook for 3 to 5 mins and do not overcook.
