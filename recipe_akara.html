Akara

Deep-fried bean cakes that are crisp on the outside and soft inside. Popular as a breakfast snack or street food in Nigeria.

Ingredients

  • Peeled beans
  • pepper
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil for frying

How To Prepare

  1. Blend peeled beans with little water until thick and smooth.
  2. Pour into a bowl, add chopped pepper and onion.
  3. Add salt to taste and mix well.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan until hot.
  5. Scoop batter into oil in small balls.
  6. Fry until golden brown on all sides.
  7. Remove and drain on paper towels.
