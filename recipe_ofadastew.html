Ofada Stew

The famous West-African party dish.

Ingredients

  • Green bell peppers
  • Green rodo
  • Onions
  • Seasoning cubes and salt
  • Palm oil
  • Assorted meats (shaki, beef, ponmo)
  • Boiled eggs
  • Locust beans (Iru)

How To Prepare

  1. Blend peppers roughly (not smooth).
  2. Bleach palm oil: heat palm oil on low heat until it changes color; donot let it smoke too much.
  3. Add chopped onions and fry till soft.
  4. Add iru and stir for 1 to 2 minutes.
  5. Pour in the blended pepper mix and fry until the oil floats on top.
  6. Add cooked meats + stock and season with cubes, salt, and crayfish if using.
  7. Cook until thick and rich.
  8. Add boiled eggs and simmer for 2 minutes before serving.
  9. Serve with Ofada rice.
