Pepperoni pizza

The famous West-African party dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups Rice
  • Tomatoes & pepper mix
  • Onions
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt

How To Prepare

  1. Blend tomatoes and pepper.
  2. Fry mixture in oil with onions.
  3. Add stock, salt and seasoning.
  4. Add rice and cook until soft.
