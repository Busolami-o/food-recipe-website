Pepperoni pizza

A cheesy, oven-baked pizza topped with tangy tomato sauce and spicy pepperoni slices. Crispy edges with a soft, flavorful center.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices

How To Prepare

  1. Roll out your dough on a floured surface.
  2. Spread pizza sauce evenly.
  3. Add mozzarella cheese and top with pepperoni.
  4. Bake in a very hot oven (200 to 250°C) for 12 to 15 mins.
