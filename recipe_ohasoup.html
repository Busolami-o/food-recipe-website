Oha Soup

The famous West-African party dish.

Ingredients

  • Oha leaves
  • Cocoyam paste or ofe thickener
  • Palm oil
  • Meat, stockfish and crayfish
  • Uziza leaves
  • Seasoning & salt

How To Prepare

  1. Boil meats until soft.
  2. Add palm oil and cocoyam paste to the pot.
  3. Add crayfish, pepper, stockfish.
  4. Add uziza leaves.
  5. Finally add oha leaves and boil for 3 mins.
