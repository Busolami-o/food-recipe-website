Seafood Pasta

The famous West-African party dish.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (spaghetti, linguine, or penne)
  • Shrimps, calamari, mussels, or fish
  • Butter or olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes or cooking cream
  • Seasoning (salt, cubes, oregano, black pepper)

How To Prepare

  1. Boil pasta with salt until firm and drain.
  2. Add garlic in butter or oil.
  3. Add seafood and stir till lightly cooked.
  4. Add tomato sauce or cream and season.
  5. Add pasta into the sauce and toss.
  6. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes and serve.
