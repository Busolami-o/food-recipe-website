Peppersoup

The famous West-African party dish.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Pepper soup spice
  • pepper
  • Onion
  • Seasoning cubes & salt
  • Scent leaves or basil

How To Prepare

  1. Wash protein properly. Season with salt, cubes, onions, pepper; boil.
  2. Add pepper soup spice once meat starts to soften.
  3. Add more water if needed and allow to cook well.
  4. Add scent leaves and boil for 2 minutes.
  5. Serve hot
