Chinese fried rice

The famous West-African party dish.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice (preferably from the previous day)
  • Carrots, peas, sweet corn
  • spring onions and Onions
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Soy sauce (optional)
  • Eggs (optional)
  • Cooked chicken, shrimp, or other protein (optional)

How To Prepare

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add beaten eggs, scramble, and remove.
  2. Add more oil, sauté onions and veggies for 2 to 3 mins.
  3. Add cooked rice and stir well.
  4. Add soy sauce (little at a time) and seasoning.
  5. Add cooked protein and scrambled eggs if using.
  6. Stir-fry for another 5 mins and serve hot.
