Chocolate cake

The famous West-African party dish.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter or oil
  • Baking powder
  • Vanilla extract

How To Prepare

  1. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl (flour, cocoa, sugar, baking powder).
  2. Add wet ingredients (eggs, milk, melted butter, vanilla).
  3. Mix until smooth and lump-free.
  4. Grease baking pan and pour in batter.
  5. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let cool before frosting or serving.
