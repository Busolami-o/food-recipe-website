Beef burger

Juicy, seasoned beef patties sandwiched in soft buns with fresh vegetables and cheese. A classic comfort food loved worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese slices
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion
  • Oil or butter

How To Prepare

  1. Form minced beef into patties; season both sides.
  2. Pan-sear patties until cooked.
  3. Place cheese on patty to melt.
  4. Toast buns lightly in butter.
  5. Assemble: bottom bun → lettuce → patty → tomato → onion → top bun.
