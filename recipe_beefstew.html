Nigerian Beef Stew

The famous West-African party dish.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Blended Tomatoes,pepper and onions
  • Tomato paste
  • Seasoning cubes and salt
  • Vegetable oil
  • Garlic/ginger

How To Prepare

  1. Season beef and boil.
  2. Heat oil, fry paste for 2 mins, then add pepper blend.
  3. Fry until thick and no longer sour.
  4. Add beef ,stock, season again, simmer 10 mins.
