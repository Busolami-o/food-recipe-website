Ogbono Soup

A traditional Nigerian soup thickened with ogbono seeds, giving it a smooth, slightly slimy texture. Cooked with meats, fish, and leafy vegetables, it’s delicious with pounded yam or fufu.

Ingredients

  • Ogbono powder
  • Palm oil
  • Meat, fish, stockfish
  • Ugu or bitterleaf
  • Seasoning & salt
  • Pepper

How To Prepare

  1. Boil meat.
  2. Heat palm oil lightly, add ogbono and melt.
  3. Add meat stock gradually while stirring to thicken.
  4. Add meats, pepper, crayfish.
  5. Add vegetables and boil for 3mins.
