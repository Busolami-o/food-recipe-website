Grilled fish

Whole fish seasoned and grilled to perfection, with crispy skin and tender flesh. Served with sides, it’s a flavorful, healthy choice.

Ingredients

  • Tilapia or catfish
  • Pepper mix (blended pepper, onion, garlic, ginger)
  • Seasoning & salt
  • Vegetable oil
  • Lemon (optional)

How To Prepare

  1. Clean fish and make slits to allow seasoning in.
  2. Rub pepper mix generously over and inside fish.
  3. Marinate 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  4. Serve with sliced onions or lemon.
