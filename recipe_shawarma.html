Shawarma

The famous West-African party dish.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Shawarma bread/wrap
  • Cabbage and carrot
  • Ketchup, mayonnaise, chili sauce
  • Sausages

How To Prepare

  1. Season chicken and pan-fry or grill till done. Slice into strips.
  2. Fry THE Sausages.
  3. Mix mayo + ketchup + chili to form shawarma sauce.
  4. Spread sauce on the wrap.
  5. Add chicken, sausages, cabbage, carrot.
  6. Wrap it up tightly and toast in a pan.
